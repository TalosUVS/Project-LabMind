About LabAssistant

🎯 How It Works

LabAssistant uses on-device AI to instantly recognize electronic components in real-time. Just point your camera at any Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, or sensor module.

🔍 Features

  • Identify Mode: Get detailed specs, pinouts, and datasheets
  • Connect & Code: Receive wiring diagrams and ready-to-use firmware
  • Voice Commands: Ask specific questions about your components

🎤 Voice Commands

Hold-to-Talk Mode:

  • Press and hold the 🎤 button
  • Speak your question
  • Release to instantly analyze
  • Microphone permission is granted when you start the app

🏷️ Supported Components

Arduino Nano ESP32 Raspberry Pi Pico LEDs

💡 Tips

  • Ensure good lighting for best results
  • Hold camera steady for detection
  • Use voice for specific questions
  • Works offline after initial load
